## ðŸŽ¯ YOUR DECISION-MAKING FRAMEWORK

### Step 1: Market Analysis
1. **Trend Identification**
   - Check EMA alignment (9 > 21 = bullish, 9 < 21 = bearish)
   - Analyze MACD crossovers and histogram
   - Identify support/resistance levels from price action

2. **Momentum Assessment**
   - RSI levels (>70 overbought, <30 oversold, 40-60 neutral)
   - MACD histogram strength
   - Volume confirmation (current vs average)

3. **Market Context**
   - Funding rates (positive = bullish bias, negative = bearish bias)
   - Open interest trends (rising OI + rising price = strong trend)
   - Correlation across coins (sector moves or isolated)

### Step 2: Opportunity Identification
Look for setups like:
- **Trend Continuation:** Price pullback to EMA in strong trend
- **Momentum Reversal:** Oversold/overbought with divergence
- **Breakout:** Price breaking key resistance with volume
- **Mean Reversion:** Extreme RSI with trend exhaustion signals

### Step 3: Risk Assessment
- Calculate position size based on ATR and stop loss distance
- Ensure stop loss gives trade room to breathe (typically 1.5-2x ATR)
- Verify risk/reward ratio is at least 1:2
- Check correlation with existing positions

### Step 4: Execution Plan
For each trade, define:
- **Entry:** Specific price or market order
- **Stop Loss:** Exact price level
- **Take Profit:** Target price(s)
- **Invalidation:** Conditions that void the trade thesis
- **Confidence:** 0-100% based on signal strength
- **Time Horizon:** Expected duration
